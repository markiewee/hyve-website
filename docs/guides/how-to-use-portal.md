Welcome to the Hyve Member Portal! This guide shows you how to do everything you need as a resident.

欢迎使用 Hyve 会员门户！本指南将向您展示如何完成所有日常操作。

## Logging In / 登录

1. Go to **hyve.sg/portal/login**
2. Enter your **username** (given by your property manager) and **password**

> **提示：** 如果忘记密码，点击 **"忘记密码？"** 并输入您的电子邮件来接收重置链接。

## Paying Rent / 支付租金

Your rent is due on the **1st of each month**.

1. From your **Dashboard**, look at the **Billing Overview** section at the top
2. You'll see your **Monthly Rent**, **Other Charges**, and **Total Due**
3. Click **"View Billing & Pay"** to go to the Billing page
4. Under **Rent Payments**, find the current month
5. To pay via **bank transfer**:
   - Bank: **DBS Bank**
   - Account Name: **Makery Pte Ltd**
   - Account Number: **DBS 072-905765-8**
   - Reference: **Your name + room code** (e.g. "John Doe CP-PR1")
6. To pay via **Stripe** (credit/debit card): Click **"Pay"** button → you'll be redirected to Stripe checkout (4% processing fee applies)

> **Important:** Late payments incur a fee of **SGD 5 per day** after the 5th of the month.

> **重要提示：** 逾期付款将在每月5日后产生每天 **SGD 5** 的滞纳金。

## Paying Other Charges / 支付其他费用

If your admin has created additional charges (stamping fee, damage, etc.):

1. Go to **Billing** page
2. Scroll down to **"Other Charges"** section
3. Each pending charge shows the description, amount, and due date
4. Click **"Invoice"** to download a printable invoice
5. Click **"Pay"** to pay via Stripe
6. After payment, click **"Receipt"** to download your receipt

## Reporting an Issue / 报告问题

Something broken? Here's how to get it fixed:

1. Click **"Issues"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Report Issue"** button
3. **Self-Diagnostic First:** Select what's wrong:
   - **AC** — check batteries, circuit breaker, mode settings
   - **Plumbing** — check water heater, pressure, toilet
   - **Electrical** — check switches, circuit breaker
   - **Furniture** — check screws, drawers
   - **Cleaning** — check cleaning schedule
   - **Other** — WiFi, noise, keys
4. Follow the troubleshooting steps. Click **"Fixed!"** if it's resolved
5. If not fixed, click **"Still broken"** until you reach **"Submit a ticket"**
6. Fill in:
   - **Category** (pre-selected from step 3)
   - **Location** (My Room, Kitchen, Bathroom, etc.)
   - **Description** (explain the problem in detail)
   - **Photos** (optional but recommended — take a photo of the issue)
7. Click **"Submit Issue"**
8. You'll see your ticket in the Issues list with its status

### Ticket Statuses / 工单状态
| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **OPEN** | Submitted, waiting for response |
| **IN PROGRESS** | Being worked on |
| **RESOLVED** | Fixed! |

### Withdrawing a Ticket / 撤回工单
If you submitted by mistake or the issue resolved itself:
1. Go to **Issues** page
2. Find your ticket
3. Click **"Withdraw"**
4. Confirm when prompted

## Uploading Documents / 上传文件

Need to upload your stamping certificate, receipt, or ID copy?

1. Click **"Documents"** in the sidebar
2. Under **"Upload Document"**, select:
   - **Document Type:** Stamping Certificate, Receipt, ID Document, or Other
   - **Title:** (optional) e.g. "March 2026 Stamping"
3. Click the file upload button and select your file (PDF, JPEG, PNG)
4. Click **"Upload Document"**
5. Your document appears in the **"All Documents"** list below

## Changing Your Password / 修改密码

1. Click **"Settings"** in the sidebar
2. Scroll to **"Change Password"**
3. Enter your **New Password** (minimum 8 characters)
4. Enter it again in **Confirm Password**
5. Click **"Change Password"**

---

## Updating Your Email / 更新电子邮件

1. Click **"Settings"** in the sidebar
2. Under **"Email Address"**, enter your new email
3. Click **"Update Email"**
4. Check your new email inbox for a verification link
5. Click the link to confirm

> **Note:** If you see a yellow banner saying "Set up your email", it means you're using a temporary login. Please add your real email to receive important notifications.

> **注意：** 如果看到黄色提示"设置您的电子邮件"，表示您正在使用临时登录。请添加您的真实电子邮件以接收重要通知。

1. Click **"Settings"** in the sidebar

## Switching Language / 切换语言

The portal supports **English** and **中文 (Chinese)**.

1. Look at the **bottom of the sidebar** (left panel)
2. Find the **EN | 中文** toggle
3. Click **中文** for Chinese or **EN** for English
4. The page will switch immediately
5. Your preference is saved — it will remember next time you log in

Keys are provided during move-in. Contact your property manager if you need a replacement.

请提前**1个月**书面通知管理处。检查后14天内退还押金。
