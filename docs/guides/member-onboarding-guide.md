## 1. Logging In / 登录

Your Hyve admin will create an account for you and provide a **username** and **password**.

1. Open your browser and go to **[hyve.sg/portal/login](https://hyve.sg/portal/login)**
2. Enter your **username** (not email) and **password**
3. Click **Log In**

> **Tip / 提示:** Bookmark the login page for quick access. If you forget your password, contact your admin — they can reset it for you from the portal.

Once logged in, you will be taken to the **Onboarding** screen. Complete each step in order to activate your account.

## Step 1: Personal Details / 个人资料

Fill in your personal information so we can set up your tenancy records.

| Field | 字段 | Required? |
|-------|------|-----------|
| Full Name | 全名 | Yes |
| Email Address | 电子邮件 | No |
| Phone Number | 电话号码 | Yes |
| Nationality | 国籍 | No |
| Date of Birth | 出生日期 | No |
| Emergency Contact Name | 紧急联系人姓名 | No |
| Emergency Contact Phone | 紧急联系人电话 | No |

**How to complete:**

1. Enter your **full legal name** as it appears on your ID
2. Enter your **phone number** — select your country code from the dropdown (Singapore +65 is the default)
3. Fill in the remaining fields
4. Click **Save & Continue**

> **Tip / 提示:** Your phone number is used for important tenancy communications. Make sure it is correct and reachable.

## Step 2: ID Verification / 身份验证

We need to verify your identity for the tenancy records.

### Singaporean Citizens & PRs / 新加坡公民与永久居民

- Upload your **NRIC** (front and back)
- Enter your NRIC number

### Foreigners / 外国人

- Upload your **passport** (photo page)
- Upload your **work pass / employment pass / student pass**
- Enter your pass number and **expiry date**

> **Important / 重要:** Your pass expiry date is tracked by the system. You will receive a reminder when your pass is nearing expiry so you can update it.

**How to complete:**

1. Take a clear photo of your document(s) or scan them
2. Upload the image(s) using the upload button
3. Fill in the required ID/pass number fields
4. Click **Save & Continue**

> **Tip / 提示:** Make sure photos are clear and all text is readable. Blurry images may be rejected.

## Step 3: Sign Agreement / 签署协议

Your Licence Agreement (tenancy agreement) will be prepared by the admin and sent to you for signing.

**If the agreement is ready:**

1. Review the agreement document carefully
2. Draw your signature using the signature pad
3. Click **Sign**
4. The admin will then counter-sign to fully execute the agreement

**If the agreement is not ready yet:**

- You may click **Skip for now** and come back later
- The admin will notify you when the agreement is ready for signing
- You can continue with the remaining onboarding steps

> **Tip / 提示:** You can also view your signed agreement later from the **Documents** page in the portal.

## After Onboarding / 入住后

### Dashboard / 仪表板

Your home page shows:

- Current billing status and upcoming rent
- AC usage hours and remaining allowance
- Recent announcements from admin (displayed as a ticker)
- Quick links to documents and issues

### Pay Rent / 支付租金

1. Go to **Billing** from the sidebar
2. Find your current month's rent
3. Click **Pay Now** to pay via Stripe, or transfer manually to the bank account on file
4. Upload a receipt if paying by bank transfer

> **Note / 注意:** Rent is due on the 1st. A late fee is applied after the 5th of each month.

### Report an Issue / 报告问题

1. Go to **Issues** from the sidebar
2. Click **Report Issue**
3. Select a **category**: AC, Plumbing, Electrical, Furniture, Cleaning, or Other
4. Select a **location**: My Room, Kitchen, Living Room, Bathroom (Shared), Corridor, Laundry Area, or Other
5. Describe the issue in detail
6. Upload photos if helpful
7. Click **Submit**

Your ticket will be tracked. You will receive updates when the status changes.

### Upload Documents / 上传文件

1. Go to **Documents** from the sidebar
2. View your signed agreements, stamping certificates, and receipts
3. Upload new documents if requested by the admin

### Change Password / 更改密码

1. Go to **Settings** from the sidebar
2. Enter your new password
3. Click **Update**

### Change Profile Details / 更改个人资料

1. Go to **Settings** from the sidebar
2. Update your email, phone, or other details
3. Click **Save**

## FAQ / 常见问题

### How do I reset my password? / 如何重置密码？

Contact your admin. They can reset your password from the portal. Alternatively, go to **Settings** if you are already logged in.

### I forgot my username. / 我忘记了用户名。

Contact your admin via WhatsApp at **+65 8088 5410**. Your username was provided when your account was created.

### My agreement is not ready yet. Can I still move in? / 协议还没准备好，我能入住吗？

Yes. You can skip the agreement signing step and complete it later. The admin will notify you when the agreement is ready.

### How do I check my AC usage? / 如何查看空调使用情况？

Your AC usage is displayed on the **Dashboard**. The first 300 hours per month are included in rent. Additional usage is charged at S$0.30 per hour.

### What happens if I pay rent late? / 如果我迟交租金会怎样？

A late fee is applied after the 5th of each month. Pay on time to avoid extra charges.

### How do I report an emergency? / 如何报告紧急情况？

For emergencies (water leak, electrical hazard, security issue), **WhatsApp us immediately at +65 8088 5410**. For non-urgent issues, use the Issues page in the portal.

### Can I have guests stay overnight? / 我可以让客人过夜吗？

Guests are welcome, but overnight stays require prior notice to the property manager.
