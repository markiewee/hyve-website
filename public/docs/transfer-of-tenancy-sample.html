5. Terms of Transfer

  1. The Incoming Tenant inherits the Outgoing Tenant's existing Licence with no modification to its terms, including but not limited to: rent, deposit, remaining lease term, house rules, and all associated liabilities.
  2. The Incoming Tenant acknowledges the remaining term and agrees to honour it without reset.
  3. The Incoming Tenant assumes all obligations tied to the Licence from the Takeover Date onwards, including pending fees, outstanding maintenance obligations, and house rules compliance.
  4. The Outgoing Tenant remains liable for rent and utilities up to (but not including) the Takeover Date.
  5. The Security Deposit originally paid by the Outgoing Tenant shall be refunded by Lazybee within 7 working days of the Incoming Tenant: (a) signing this agreement, (b) paying their own Security Deposit and first month's rent, and (c) officially moving in.
  6. A final inspection shall be conducted before the Takeover Date. Any deductions for unpaid rent, damage beyond normal wear and tear, missing inventory, or required deep cleaning shall be itemised and deducted from the Outgoing Tenant's Security Deposit refund.

6. Outgoing Tenant's Obligations

  1. Hand over the Room in good condition on the Takeover Date.
  2. Return all keys, access cards, and inventory items to Lazybee or to the Incoming Tenant as agreed.
  3. Ensure the Incoming Tenant has reviewed and agreed to this Agreement and the original Licence.
  4. Answer any reasonable queries from the Incoming Tenant regarding the Room, the property, and the existing Licence.

7. Incoming Tenant's Acknowledgement

By signing below, the Incoming Tenant acknowledges that they have:

  1. Read and understood the original Licence Agreement.
  2. Accepted the Room in its current condition as inspected.
  3. Accepted all terms of the existing Licence, including the remaining term, rent, and all liabilities.
  4. Paid (or agreed to pay on or before the Takeover Date) the Security Deposit and first month's rent to Lazybee.
